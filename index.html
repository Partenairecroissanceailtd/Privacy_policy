1. Information We Collect

Direct Collection: We do not directly collect, harvest, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address on our own servers.

Because our organization is established in the United Kingdom, we process data in compliance with the UK Data Protection Act and UK GDPR. Even though we do not directly collect personal data, you have the following rights regarding third-party tracking:

TradeQuote Pro is a utility application designed strictly for commercial and professional trade usage by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will take immediate steps to delete it.
